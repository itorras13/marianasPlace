About Mariana's Place

Mariana's Place is a multisensory room that contains multi-colored bubble tubes, fibre optic lights and tactile toys designed to stimulate the senses of young patients with cancer, cerebral palsy, neurological disorders, and other physical illnesses and injuries. We want to give kids that need occupational and physical therapy the opportunity to receive it using these rooms. We hope to promote the benefit of such rooms while opening them in hopsitals around the nation.
